# 🎥 How to Upload an Unlisted YouTube Video

## Step-by-Step Guide

### Step 1: Record & Upload to YouTube
1. Record your course lesson video
2. Go to [YouTube Studio](https://studio.youtube.com)
3. Click **Create** → **Upload videos**
4. Select your video file
5. Fill in title and description

### Step 2: Set Video to Unlisted ⭐ IMPORTANT
**This is crucial for the course platform to work properly!**

1. After uploading, click **MORE OPTIONS**
2. Under **Visibility**, select **🔗 Unlisted** (NOT Private)
   - ✅ **Unlisted** - Anyone with the link can watch
   - ❌ **Private** - Only you can watch (won't work with our platform)
   - ❌ **Public** - Anyone can find it on YouTube
3. Click **SAVE**

### Step 3: Get Your Video URL
1. Once your video is live, click on it
2. Copy the URL from the browser address bar
   - Format: `https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VIDEO_ID`
   - Or: `https://youtu.be/VIDEO_ID`

### Step 4: Add to Your Course Platform
1. Go to your **Course Dashboard**
2. Click **📹 Video** button on your course
3. Select **Link YouTube/Vimeo**
4. Paste the video URL
5. Click **Save Video URL**

✅ **Done!** Your video is now linked to your course.

---

## Alternative: Direct Upload to Platform

If you prefer to upload directly without YouTube:

1. Click **📹 Video** on your course
2. Select **Upload Video File**
3. Click the upload area
4. Select your MP4, WebM, or Ogg file
5. Wait for upload to complete

**Benefits of Direct Upload:**
- ✅ Complete privacy control
- ✅ No YouTube account needed
- ✅ Faster access
- ✅ Better control over video quality

**Considerations:**
- Max file size: 2GB
- Compression recommended for large files
- Slower initial upload but smoother playback afterward

---

## Video Settings Checklist

Before using your video in the course:

- [ ] Video is uploaded to YouTube or ready to upload directly
- [ ] **YouTube videos are set to UNLISTED** (not private)
- [ ] Video title is clear and descriptive
- [ ] Description includes lesson summary
- [ ] Video quality is good (720p or higher recommended)
- [ ] Audio is clear and easy to understand
- [ ] Video duration is appropriate (aim for 5-15 minutes per lesson)

## Troubleshooting

### "Video is private - can't watch"
→ YouTube video privacy is set to Private
→ Solution: Change to Unlisted in YouTube settings

### "Video won't load"
→ Check internet connection
→ Try a different browser
→ Verify YouTube video is actually Unlisted

### "Upload is very slow"
→ Compress your video file first
→ Try uploading during off-peak hours
→ Check your internet speed

### "Video uploaded but preview shows black screen"
→ Wait a few seconds and refresh the page
→ Try a different browser
→ Check browser console for errors (F12)

## Pro Tips 💡

1. **Use YouTube for Public Courses** - Easy to share, unlisted videos stay private
2. **Use Direct Upload for Premium Content** - Complete privacy control
3. **Test Your Videos** - Always preview before sharing with students
4. **Keep Videos Short** - Break lessons into 5-15 minute videos
5. **Add Captions** - YouTube has auto-captions; add custom ones for clarity
6. **Organize Your Videos** - Use clear naming: "Lesson 1 - The Promise"
